Privacy Policy for StudWork

Effective Period: 2024–2026 · Last Updated: January 2024

StudWork ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and disclose information when you use the StudWork website and services (the "Platform"). By accessing or using StudWork, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • School or educational information
  • Resume/CV and related profile details
  • Account login credentials

1.2 Non-Personal Information

We may automatically collect non-personal information such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Match students with relevant part-time job opportunities
  • Enable resume/CV uploads and employer viewing
  • Communicate updates, notifications, and support messages
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

StudWork does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With employers or recruiters when you apply for a job or make your profile visible
  • With trusted service providers who help operate the Platform (under confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of StudWork and its users

4. Data Protection and Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of online transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Control the visibility of your profile and resume
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

StudWork may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze site usage, and improve services. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

7. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites. StudWork is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage users to review their privacy policies.

8. Eligibility and Intended Users

StudWork is intended exclusively for college students, employers, and companies. By using the Platform, you confirm that you are legally capable of entering into agreements and providing accurate information. StudWork does not knowingly provide services to individuals outside this intended user group.
